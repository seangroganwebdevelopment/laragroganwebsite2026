Project

Tales & Legends in Song

A unique live music experience inspired by little-known legends from Luxembourg’s medieval past.

Why these legends — and why now?

What do centuries-old legends have to do with our lives today? Quite a lot, it turns out. Through storytelling and original songs, Lara brings old stories into the present, showing how they still speak to us today.

From figures such as Princess Elisabeth of Dudelange, to the restless soul of Veit from Echternach, the painful history of the Witch Persecutions, and the eerie legend of the Werewolf of Bettembourg — these characters and tales are brought to life through song in a captivating musical journey.

Themes

This project is about more than history. Themes such as courage, identity, fear of change, exclusion, and the complexity of human emotions are woven into each song — inviting listeners to reflect on challenges we all share.

At its heart

Tales & Legends in Song is more than a concert — it is a shared experience of storytelling and connection, linking past and present and rediscovering timeless human experiences.
